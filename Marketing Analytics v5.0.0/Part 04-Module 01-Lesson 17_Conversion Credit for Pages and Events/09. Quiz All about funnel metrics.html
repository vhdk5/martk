09. Quiz: All about funnel metrics

Funnel conversion rate refers to the number of ______________ in which the funnel was entered and the goal destination page was reached.
SOLUTION: Sessions

(True or False) Abandonment rate is calculated for all goals, whether or not a goal funnel has been configured.
SOLUTION: False

(True or False) Funnel Conversion Rate and Funnel Abandonment Rate should always add up to 100%.
SOLUTION: False

(True or False) A higher funnel step in a funnel always reports at least as many completions as a lower step, even if all of those funnel step completions did not actually occur.
SOLUTION: True

Which of these is retroactive when applied to data set?
SOLUTION: Sequence Segments

(True or False) Sequence segments can only be based on page views.
SOLUTION: False
