27. Quiz: Price Premium

SOLUTION: Benchmark

(Fill in the blank) The goal of Price Premium is to measure your price, against the ___________ average price.
SOLUTION: Market

(Fill in the blank) Price Premium is sometimes referred to as ___________ Price
SOLUTION: Relative
