21. Quiz: NPS

In Marketing, NPS stands for:
SOLUTION: Net Promoter Score

NPS uses a score between:
SOLUTION: 1 to 10

A score of a 9 or 10 indicates a customer is a:
SOLUTION: Promoter

A score of 7 or 8 indicates a customer is a :

SOLUTION: Passive

A score of less than 6 means a customer is a:

SOLUTION: Detractor
