08. Quiz: Getting Oriented in Google Analytics

Your Turn: Getting Oriented in Google Analytics

Now that we have a bit of an idea of how the Google Analytics interface is structured, let’s take a look at the data.

You can play around on the overview page of the Google Merchandise store and check out each statement that follows. Then select whether the statements are true or false. We will walk through the answers one by one. We will go through 4 statements to practice.

Note that in order to answer these questions, you will need to work with the Google Analytics account for the Google Merchandise Store you just got access to. I strongly suggest you try this out as you will find that the best way to learn how Google Analytics works is by practicing in a real account.

Question 1

From January 1st until June 30th of 2016, the average number of sessions per month increased.
SOLUTION: True

Question 2

The proportion of new users (vs. returning users) was lower in June 2016 than it was in January 2016.
SOLUTION: False

Question 3

In the first half of 2016, excluding "(not set)", most sessions on the site were from visitors from New York.
SOLUTION: True

Question 4

44.84% of the sessions from mobile in the first half of the year were through devices with an iOS operating system.
SOLUTION: True
