03. Accessing the Google Analytics Account

Accessing Google Analytics Account

The Google Analytics account for the Merchandise Store is a fully functional account, and as a student, you can access it, so you can get hands-on experience analyzing the data. Access is very simple and this process simultaneously sets up a Google Analytics for you if you don't already have one.

  1. You’ll need a Google account (you may have this if you use Gmail, etc.). If you don’t have one, click here and sign up.

  2. Sign in to your Google Account

  3. Click this link to Google’s page with an overview of the demo account:
    Sign Up to Access Demo Account

  4. On this page, click on the link “Access Demo Account” (circled in red)
