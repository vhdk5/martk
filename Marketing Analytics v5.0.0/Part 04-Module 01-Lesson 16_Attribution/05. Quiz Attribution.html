05. Quiz: Attribution

Which of these is the default, standard attribution model in Google Analytics?
SOLUTION: Last non-direct click

What is the default length of the campaign time-out window?
SOLUTION: Six months

(True or False) If a user’s first session is attributed to Paid Search, their next session will also be attributed to Paid Search if no no new referral or campaign is present and the timing of the session falls within the campaign timeout window.
SOLUTION: True

To get a clearer breakdown of the original sessions attributed to a channel versus the the subsequent direct sessions that channel is also receiving attribution credit for, which secondary dimensions should you apply to a channel report?
SOLUTION: Direct Session

If no referring data is present on the inbound session and no campaign data is associated with the user within the campaign expiration window, the traffic will be counted in which channel?
SOLUTION: Direct
