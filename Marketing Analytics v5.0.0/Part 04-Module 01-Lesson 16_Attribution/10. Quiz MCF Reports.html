10. Quiz: MCF Reports

(True or False) The Multi-Channel Funnel Reports in GA can help show how different marketing efforts overlap and support each other
SOLUTION: True

(True or False) When using the MCF reports to try and determine what’s driving conversions, it’s a good idea to look at multiple kinds of conversions at the same time. What is the default length of the campaign time-out window?
SOLUTION: False

In order to examine the time-lag of conversion, what can you adjust in the MCF report?
SOLUTION: Lookback window

(True or False) To compare how specific attributes of a conversion compare to one another, as as to your overall conversions characteristics, you can apply Conversion Segments to the MCF reports.
SOLUTION: True

Which attribution model, available in the Model Comparison Tool, gives proportional credit across all touch points within the look-back window, equally and with no consideration to position or recency?
SOLUTION: Linear

(True or False) In the position-based attribution model, the first interactions are given more credit for conversions than the rest of the interactions that led to it.

SOLUTION: False
