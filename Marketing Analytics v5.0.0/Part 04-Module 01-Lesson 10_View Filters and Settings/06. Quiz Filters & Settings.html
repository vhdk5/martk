06. Quiz: Filters & Settings

As a minimum, you should have at least how many views for each for each of your GA properties?
SOLUTION: 3

(True or False) One of the primary differences between a custom segment and a dedicated, filtered view is that the filtered view is retroactive while the custom segment can only show data from the point of it’s creation onward.
SOLUTION: False

(True or False) GA automatically filters out your organization’s internal data based on IP addresses.
SOLUTION: False

Which of these is not a good reason to consider filtering your organization’s internal traffic out of your GA dataset?

SOLUTION: Your internal data should always live in a dedicated view
