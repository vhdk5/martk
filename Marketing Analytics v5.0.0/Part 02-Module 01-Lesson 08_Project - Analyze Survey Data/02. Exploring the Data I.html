02. Exploring the Data I

Cleaning Up The Data

Although you do not need to follow these for setting up the dataset, These are some suggestions:

  1. All questions where the answer lives across multiple columns, combine into a single column and remove the original columns. Keep only the combined column.

  2. Change all the column names to have no spaces, but still be informative. This isn't necessary, but just a recommendation. Depending on what you do with the data in the future having spaces or special characters in the column names may not work nicely. You will see this in the next content on SQL.

  3. Change the birthdate column to an age column by finding the number of days from that date to today, and then dividing by 365.

Reasonability Check

Another imperative part of analyzing data is understanding what parts of our data are reasonable, and then using the data appropriately for answering our questions of interest. Before looking at our data, which of the following claims seem reasonable.
SOLUTION:
  • The most frequent 'Highest Degree Earned' is a Nanodegree Program.
  • The mean 'average hours worked' on a project exceeds the number of hours in a day.
  • More than 50% of students spend more than 24 hours on 'average for projects'.
  • The maximum 'number of books read or listened to in a year' exceeds the number of days in a year.

What was the most frequent "Highest Degree Earned"?
SOLUTION: Masters

One of the most important aspects of analyzing data is being clear about what you can and cannot say. For each of the below statements, analyze the data to see if the claim is true. Select all of the below that are true based on the survey data.
SOLUTION:
  • The average number of hours of sleep of students exceeds the number of hours in a day.
  • The maximum number of hours spent sitting in a day exceeds the number of hours in a day.
  • The youngest participants are less than a year old.
  • The mean 'average hours worked' on a project exceeds the number of hours in a day.
  • The maximum 'number of books read or listened to in a year' exceeds the number of days in a year.

What is the most common Nanodegree Program students of the survey are taking?
SOLUTION: Deep Learning Foundations
