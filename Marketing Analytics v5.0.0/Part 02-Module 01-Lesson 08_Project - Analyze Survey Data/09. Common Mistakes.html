When can you report the measures of center and spread?

The most common issue we see is students reporting measures of center and spread across categorical statistics. Here is an example graph:

SOLUTION: You cannot compute the mean or standard deviation for the above, because education is categorical.

The graph was based on categorical data and students are regularly reporting the mean, median, mode, standard deviation, and range of this sort of data.

This is incorrect

This data is in an arbitrary order on the x-axis so there is no definite center to this data so none of the measures of center apply. The measures of spread also rely on the data having an order and a center.
