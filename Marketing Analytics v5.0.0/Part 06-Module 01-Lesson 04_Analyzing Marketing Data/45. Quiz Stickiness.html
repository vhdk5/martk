Match the stickiness ratio for the months of January through April.

Smoothie Rocks now goes ahead and designs a mobile app for its customers. The app allows customers to find nearby Smoothie Rocks locations, check out the menu options, get coupons and place orders online.

Continue using the data in the BAND Stickiness Quiz file to answer the question below.

The investors of Smoothie Rocks want to know if the mobile app is showing customers engagement is high. They want to know if the last three months of data (April, May, June) show that Smoothie Rocks mobile customers have been coming to the site at least half of each month. What would you tell them?
SOLUTION: No, the stickiness ratio was above 0.50 only for the month of June.
