** What does the average deal size indicate?**
SOLUTION: The average bookings size a sales person has closed the deals for

A sales person for a company has won three deals with three big corporate accounts. The value of these deals is $500,000, $850,000 and $560,000. ** What is the total bookings for this sales person?**
SOLUTION: $1,910,000
