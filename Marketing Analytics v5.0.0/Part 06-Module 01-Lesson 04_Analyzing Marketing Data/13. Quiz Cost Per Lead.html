13. Quiz: Cost Per Lead

Quiz

Continue using the Smoothie Rocks CSV file named BAND_MarketingMetric_Quizzes_A. We have provided a copy in the Resources below if you need it again. Calculate the necessary metrics to answer the quiz questions below.

** Which of the ad platforms had the highest Cost Per Lead?**
SOLUTION:
  • Facebook

** Which of the ad platforms had the lowest Cost Per Lead?**
SOLUTION:
  • Google Display

Reflect

QUESTION:

Given the marketing metrics you have calculated thus far for the Smoothie Rocks ad, which ad platform would you recommend your marketing team revisit or tweak the ad for? Think about the number of clicks, CPC and CPL as you examine the data.

ANSWER:

Our recommendation for the marketing team would be that Facebook appears to have the highest Cost Per Lead of $5.83 while getting 450 clicks. For a comparable number of clicks (497), Google YouTube not only has a lower Cost Per Lead ($1.18). Facebook also appears to have a high Cost Per Click of $0.70 compared to Google YouTube ($0.23).

Recap:

  • ** Cost Per Lead (CPL)** = Cost of advertising on the source platform / Total number of leads
  • CPL is an indicator of the cost effectiveness of the ad platform and a useful tool to compare and strategize about which marketing platforms yielded more leads.
