44. Calculating: Stickiness

The following video describes how to calculate the Monthly Active Users, Daily Active Users and Stickiness Ratio.

Stickiness V1

Interpretation of Stickiness Ratio

  • A 50% stickiness ratio indicates the average customer used the online app or website 15 of the 30 days in that month. In contrast, a .01 stickiness ratio indicates the average customer used the online app or website only one day that month.
  • A higher stickiness ratio indicates the website or online app is engaging the customers.
