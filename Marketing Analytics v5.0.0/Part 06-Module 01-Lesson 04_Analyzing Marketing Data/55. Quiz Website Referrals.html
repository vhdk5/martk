55. Quiz: Website Referrals

(Fill in the blank) Every visitor to your site, came from somewhere. We call this a “_________”
SOLUTION: Referral

True/False: Referral traffic data will not include any information about engagement.
SOLUTION: False

Reflect

QUESTION:

Explain what you would do if you saw a large amount of referral traffic arriving, but the average session duration (time on site) was significantly lower than your average.

ANSWER:

Example: We can look at this content and see if the visitors are getting the answer they need on our site. If they are not we can update the content, or ask the referral source to change the link to a better section of our website.
