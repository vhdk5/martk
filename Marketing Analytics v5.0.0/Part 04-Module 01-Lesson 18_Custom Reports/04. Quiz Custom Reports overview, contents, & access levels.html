04. Quiz: Custom Reports overview, contents, & access levels

Which of the following is not true of Custom Reports in Google Analytics?
SOLUTION: You can only use Custom Dimensions in Custom Reports

(True or False) Custom Reports can be seen by anyone with access to the view in which they are created.
SOLUTION: False

To create and view a Custom Report, what level of access rights do you need?
SOLUTION: Create and Analyze

(True or False) Custom Reports alter the underlying data on which they are based.
SOLUTION: False
