23. Quiz: Customer Segmenting

What is a consumer segment?
SOLUTION: A sub-group of consumers with many similarities

What attribute is true of a good customer segment?

SOLUTION: Customers are very similar within their segment, and dissimilar to other segments

(Fill in the blank) You can have segments based on _________ of customers or based on simple business _______.
SOLUTION: Attributes, Rules

True/False: If we can find all the people that are alike, we can assume that their behaviors are also very much alike
SOLUTION: True

True/False: There is a one-size-fits-all customer.

SOLUTION: False

Reflect

QUESTION:

Explain why you think the average order value could change between different customer segments?

ANSWER:

Example: Because we could have high-value customers, that order lots of items from us and we could have low-value customers that only order one item. We may have high-income consumers and low-income consumers, and their behaviors will vary.

True/False: Segmentation gives us granular data that is not actionable
SOLUTION: False
