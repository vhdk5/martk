11. Quiz: Business Story

(Fill in the blank): Most importantly, our analysis must be ______________.
SOLUTION: Actionable

Which are the two questions our business story focuses on?
SOLUTION:
  • What relevant actionable segments do we have access to?
  • Which segments should we pursue?

Your company sells backpacks. It’s a month before school starts. Which customer segment would you want to pursue?
SOLUTION: Parents of Students

Free response:

QUESTION:

Come up with three different types of customer segments for a company that sells backpacks, and describe when that customer segment is most actionable.

ANSWER:

Examples:

High School Students. Students in high school likely have influence over buying decisions, and it would be important to position the product in front of them.

People Interested in Hiking. Just before the good weather arrives, people that like to hike might be looking for new backpacks.

Business Travelers. This segment is also very actionable, likely anytime after the school season starts because business travelers will be looking for backpacks year round.
