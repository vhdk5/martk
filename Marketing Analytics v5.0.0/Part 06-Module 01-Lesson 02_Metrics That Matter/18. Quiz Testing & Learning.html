18. Quiz: Testing & Learning

The testing & learning plan is what step in the analytic brief:
SOLUTION: 3rd/Last

Free Form

QUESTION:

Describe in 2-3 sentences why we use the testing & learning plan?

ANSWER:

Example: The testing and learning plan helps us organize our thoughts around what we should study further, what additional data we need, and what ideas we should try. We use it to think through the marketing campaign so we understand what else we need to analyze and pull data on for the campaign to be successful. Not every analytics project is straightforward, and we might need to pull data that isn’t a metric -- such as what shows are running on a TV station, or which radio stations are most popular.

(True or False): You can skip the testing & learning plan stage of the analytic brief
SOLUTION: False
