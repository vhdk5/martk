05. Quiz: Measuring Across the Journey

When the entire value of the customer’s conversion is placed on the channel that most recently delivered that customer, it’s said to be what kind of attribution?
SOLUTION: Last Click

(True or False): The customer journey is always a linear journey
SOLUTION: False

(Fill in the blank) When we set out to measure the customer journey, our goal is to look at the most _____________ path.
SOLUTION: common

What do we mean by the reference experience as it relates to the customer journey?

SOLUTION: how the customer ideally wants to navigate
