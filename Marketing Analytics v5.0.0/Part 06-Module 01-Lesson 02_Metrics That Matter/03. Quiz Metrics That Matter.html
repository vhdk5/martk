03. Quiz: Metrics That Matter

In the Pirate Metrics Acronym AARRR, what does the first A refer to:
SOLUTION: Acquisition

In the Pirate Metrics Acronym AARRR, what does the second A refer to:
SOLUTION: Activation

In the Pirate Metrics Acronym AARRR, what does the first R refer to:
SOLUTION: Retention

In the Pirate Metrics Acronym AARRR, what does the second R refer to:
SOLUTION: Referral

In the Pirate Metrics Acronym AARRR, what does the last R refer to:
SOLUTION: Revenue
