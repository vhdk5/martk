05. Quiz: Content Popularity

Imagine you work in the Google Merchandise Store Marketing team. The design group comes to you to get a sense of the most popular types of items in the Google Merchandise Store. They want to know which items visitors of the website explore most because they want to come up with a few new products to attract users’ attention.

You decide to provide the designers with a ranking of the product categories based on their popularity during the month of August 2016. Which of the following two rankings of Google Product categories will you provide to the designers?

SOLUTION: Apparel, office, bags, electronics, drinkware, lifestyle, brands
