06. Quiz: Annotations visibility

In GA, annotations can exist in reports that contain a ____________________.
SOLUTION: Timeline

Which of these isn’t an occurrence worthy of putting an annotation in GA?
SOLUTION: Adding a user to your GA account

(True or False) It’s a best practice to enter annotations into GA on a weekly or bi-weekly basis.

SOLUTION: False

In order to view annotations for a given date, what condition must exist?
SOLUTION: It must be a date within the range of dates selected in the date range selector

(True or False) Annotations are only visible to the person who creates them.
SOLUTION: False
