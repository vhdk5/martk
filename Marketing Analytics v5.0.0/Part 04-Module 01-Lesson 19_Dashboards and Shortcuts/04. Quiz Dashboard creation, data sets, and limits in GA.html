04. Quiz: Dashboard creation, data sets, and limits in GA

Within GA, how many different ways are there to create a dashboard?
SOLUTION: Three

(True or False) Both GA Dashboards and Data Studio Dashboards can incorporate data that doesn’t come directly through Google Analytics.
SOLUTION: False

How many shared dashboards can you have for each view in GA?
SOLUTION: 50

How many widgets can each of your GA dashboard widgets contain?
SOLUTION: 12
