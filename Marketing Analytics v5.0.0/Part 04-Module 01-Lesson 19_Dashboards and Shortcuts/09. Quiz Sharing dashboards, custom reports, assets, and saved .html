09. Quiz: Sharing dashboards, custom reports, assets, and saved

(True or False) When importing dashboards from the Solutions Gallery, special care needs to be taken because it’s possible that the solution you begin with may alter the underlying reporting data that is displaying.

SOLUTION: False

(Fill in the blank) In order to share a dashboard, you need ________________access at the ________________level.
SOLUTION: collaborate, view

(True or False) Custom reports and dashboards cannot be shared outside the view in which they are created.
SOLUTION: False

Which of these is not a native format option for exporting reports from GA?
SOLUTION: DBS

A great way to share assets (like custom reports, configurations, and channel groupings) across accounts is to:
SOLUTION: Share them with yourself from one account then open them in the other account

(True or False) Saved reports cannot be shared in GA.
SOLUTION: True
