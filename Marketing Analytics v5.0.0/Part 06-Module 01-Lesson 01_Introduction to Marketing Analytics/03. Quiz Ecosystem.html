03. Quiz: Ecosystem

How many marketing analytics tools are available?
SOLUTION: Between 6,001 - 7,000

What do we call the collection of technology a company uses for its Marketing?
SOLUTION: The Marketing Technology Stack (MarTech)

What are the 5 areas of the funnel we seek to measure in Marketing?
SOLUTION: Awareness, Interest, Desire, Action, Advocacy

Select the two primary challenges we are faced with regarding our data in Marketing Analytics:
SOLUTION:
  • Where should our data come from?
  • How do we use that data?
