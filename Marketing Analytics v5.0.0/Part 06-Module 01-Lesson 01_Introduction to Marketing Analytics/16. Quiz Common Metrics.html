16. Quiz: Common Metrics

What does CTR stand for?
SOLUTION: Click Through Rate

How is CTR calculated?
SOLUTION: `# of clicks divided by the # of impressions`

What is a conversion?
SOLUTION: When a visitor takes the desired action of your marketing objective

How is the conversion rate calculated?
SOLUTION: `# of visitors divided by # who took the desired action`

What does CPM mean?

SOLUTION: Cost per thousand people reached

What does CAC or CPA stand for?
SOLUTION: The cost to acquire a customer

What does LTV or CLV stand for?
SOLUTION: Customer lifetime value

What does ROI mean?
SOLUTION: Return on Investment

What metric (or type of metric) would be associated with a social media post?
SOLUTION: Engagement

(True or False) A referral is when a user clicks to your website from another source on the web.

SOLUTION: True

(True or False) A session is when a visitor performs a search on your website.
SOLUTION: False
