18. Quiz: Business Goals

(Fill in the blank) All business objectives should follow the_________goal format:
SOLUTION: SMART

(Fill in the blank) All business objectives must be _________:
SOLUTION: Measurable

Which of the following are well-formed business objectives?
(Select all that apply)
SOLUTION:
  • Secure 20 sales by end of Q1
  • Generate $50,000 in revenue by Q2
  • Generate 5,000 referral visitors in May
  • By Q3, Reduce churn by 10%

(True or False): Business goals should be time-bound

SOLUTION: True
