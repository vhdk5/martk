30. Quiz: Values of Analytics

(Fill in the blank) Every marketing initiative will begin with your __________ metrics.

SOLUTION: Success

(Fill in the blank) Do not use or report metrics based on __ or incomplete data.
SOLUTION: Inaccurate

(Fill in the blank) Don’t use _______ metrics just look busy or because someone likes to see marketing numbers.
SOLUTION: Vanity

(Fill in the blank) Everyone has an opinion, but ______ are the judge of results or success.
SOLUTION: Metrics

(Fill in the blank) When the analytics output conflicts with your ______, choose the analytics.
SOLUTION: Beliefs

(Fill in the blank) When the analytics reveal _______ results, take credit.
SOLUTION: Good

(Fill in the blank) When the analytics reveal poor results, take _________.
SOLUTION: Responsibility

(Fill in the blank) Don’t use the output of the analytics process punitively, only to ________.
SOLUTION: Improve

(Fill in the blank) Analytics don’t make the_________, they will inform the ________ we ultimately make.
SOLUTION: Decisions, decisions

(Fill in the blank) Every marketing initiative will end by___________ the data.

SOLUTION: Reviewing
