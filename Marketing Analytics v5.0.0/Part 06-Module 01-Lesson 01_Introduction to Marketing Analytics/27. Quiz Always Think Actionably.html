27. Quiz: Always Think Actionably

(Fill in the blank) __________ are how you share your findings and socialize the recommendations.
SOLUTION: Reports

When creating slides, the first slide should contain:
SOLUTION: Overview

(Fill in the blank) It’s important to include the ______________ when preparing documents for executives.
SOLUTION: Project scope

What should be included in your methodology section?
SOLUTION: How much data was collected and from where?

What should be included in your hypothesis section?
SOLUTION: What was the desired outcome?

What should be included in your recommendations section?
SOLUTION: What should happen?
