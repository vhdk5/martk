06. Quiz: Setting up Goals

So, we learned about how goals are set up. Let’s take a look at the Goals in the Google Merchandise account, and see what we can find there.

A few goals have been set up, one of them is the “Purchase Completed” goal.

In the Google Analytics Demo account for the Google Online Merchandise Store, take a look at the “purchase Completed” goal.

Question 1

Which goal type is the ‘purchase completed’ goal:
SOLUTION: Destination Goal

Question 2

Which of the following pages is not listed as part of the funnel that leads up to the ‘purchase completed goal’?
SOLUTION: Coupons and Promotions
