03. Meet your Instructors

Meet the Instructors!

There are several instructors to support your journey into data science. Here are a few of them!

Josh Bernhard

Josh will introduce you to practical statistics and how to conduct statistical analysis using python libraries. Josh has a graduate degree in statistics and has extensive experience as a data scientist in the industry. He has worked for several firms, including KPMG and Galvanize.

Dana Sheahen

Dana will introduce you to data analysis in Microsoft Excel. She has a background in electrical engineering and computer science. She's taught courses on data analysis, artificial intelligence, robotics, and autonomous machines

** Mat Leonard **

Mat Leonard is a former physicist, research neuroscientist, and data scientist. He did his PhD and Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of California, Berkeley.

Brad Batesole

Brad is a marketing executive who has led teams of top marketers at top companies: Lynda.com, TMZ, LegalZoom, and LinkedIn. He has extensive experience from every angle, from marketing strategy and user experience to social media, paid media, and SEO.

Anke Audenaert

Anke led the development of Digital Marketing programs at Udacity. She previously ran Market Research at Yahoo!, co-founded two companies, and is on the marketing faculty at UCLA Anderson.
